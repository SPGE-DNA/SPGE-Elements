About Our School

SPGE "John Atanasoff" is a leading technical high school specializing in electronics, programming, and renewable energy. Founded in 1968, our school has been at the forefront of technical education in Bulgaria.

Our mission is to prepare the next generation of technology innovators through hands-on learning, industry partnerships, and cutting-edge facilities.
